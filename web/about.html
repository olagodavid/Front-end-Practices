About AceTv

Ace TV is a TV content producer and distributor based in Nairobi, Kenya, East Africa. We...
1. Produce Content - We create original TV content in diverse genres, such as Reality Shows, Sitcoms, Serial Dramas, Documentaries and Game Shows. We also facilitate the production of content by other producers through joint venture agreements. All the content in whose production we are involved is distributed exclusively through our channels unless otherwise arranged.
2. Distribute Content - We are the Ace TV channel, available in all of Kenya’s leading digital television broadcast platforms. Through our channel, we broadcast the content we producer as well as content produced by other parties. Beyond the TV channel, we also distribute content through our proprietory web portal and mobile applications as well as popular social platforms such as YouTube, Facebook and Twitter.
