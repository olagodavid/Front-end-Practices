About NIEF

The Nairobi International Education Fair (NIEF), a 4 day event that is held in Nairobi Kenya, provides a one-stop forum for local and international colleges and universities to engage students, school leavers, teachers and parents on careers and training programmes being offered by those institutions.

The forum will also interest teachers who are seeking to equip themselves to be able to counsel their students in career matters in their schools as well as parents who wish to know what career opportunities are available for their children.

NIEF Chairperson

What we do

Offers

NIEF offers exclusive opportunity to education institutions to directly interact face to face with thousands of aspiring students, parents, career guidance teachers and working professionals planning to pursue further studies in Kenya and abroad.

Train

NIEF brings together local and international universities and colleges to meet students, school leavers and other young people who are looking for a career and training information so as to guide and train them in making the correct choices.

Quality Output

The Education in Kenya Media platforms have been strengthened and brings together Students, School Leavers, Parents and Teachers with Colleges and Universities to receive comprehensive career information to enable them make informed career choices.

More About Us & Our Partners

The main objective of the event is to bring together local and international universities and colleges to meet students, school leavers and other young people who are looking for career and training information so that they can benefit from each other. Even the working class people who are seeking to further their Education find this forum beneficial.
Happening after the Nairobi International Education Fair, are the Regional Education & Career Fairs. For more information see the Fairs Calender.
Career seminars bringing together students, school leavers, parents, teachers, career counselors, representatives of employers in industry, colleges and universities will also be organised at those venues.

The National & Regional Education & Career Fairs are organised by Express Communications Ltd (ECL), which is the leading promoter and marketer of Education, Careers and Training Services in Kenya.
ECL has been joined by Various County Governments in partnerships with Kenya Private Schools Association (KPSA) and other Education stakeholders (local and international) to organise this events..
With endorsements from the Ministry of Education and the Kenya Private Sector Alliance (KEPSA), the Education in Kenya Media platforms have been strengthened and brings together Students, School Leavers, Parents and Teachers with Colleges and Universities to receive comprehensive career information to enable them make informed career choices.
